## Key decisions (human-locked this session)

  reincarnation/isekai.

  to a human/natural cause; thin capped ambiguity, never player magic.

  reincarnation.
- **Working title** (D-005): *Kamikakushi*.

## §1 review — round 2 (human reviewing the redrafted `docs/prd.md` §1)

- **§1.3 — combat must be a CORE pillar (not "the rare exception").** The redraft over-indexes on
  "peaceful Edo labour" and marginalises combat. **Rebalance:** combat is a **first-class core pillar** of
  the incremental RPG — **character levels, combat skills, mobs to grind, loot drops, equipment to find
  AND craft, gear progression** — present **alongside** the peaceful production economy (both are core
  loops; both/and, not peaceful-dominant). Honours the README (mobs/areas/equipment). KEEP the grounded
  mediocre-start (you START weak at combat too; the first fight is humbling; combat capacity is earned via
  training, no hidden edge) — but once unlocked, combat is a **full central system**, not a sideshow.
  **Revise Pillar 4** ("rare, dangerous exception … never a source of standing"): combat CAN earn
  standing/House Influence (clear bandits, make roads safe, defend the estate — a samurai house values
  martial service). Affects §1 (pillars + 1.3 tone + combat framing) and downstream **§2** (combat as a
  first-class system) and **§4** (combat/progression/loot/gear balance).
- **§1.5.1 — the estate must GROW + a longer, labor/combat-INTERLEAVED ladder + earlier canvas.** Rising
  through acts/chapters grows BOTH the player's own rank AND the whole house (out of debt → higher Influence
  in region & Edo), so the **estate itself visibly grows** — more land/holdings, buildings, and a **growing
  roster of members** (retainers, staff, servants, soldiers). The current 8 ranks are too narrow (labor/
  manual/local only). **Add more ranks and INTERLEAVE labor-focused and combat-focused ranks** (climb as a
  fighter/retainer too, not just a steward); labor/estate-rep ranks are early-game, combat ranks weave
  throughout; **combat earns standing**. **Expand the canvas EARLIER:** sent on **regional quests (and
  combat) BEFORE** the top ranks (Deputy / Right-hand) — decouple the personal rank ladder from the outward
  tier-canvas so region content opens mid-climb, not behind the capstone. ("Ladder opens next canvas" is OK
  in principle, but the canvas must open earlier.)
- **§1.5.1 — combat content, "search far and wide."** Rich, varied mobs/enemies & quests: field/forest
  pests & animals, bandits, ronin, smugglers, wildlife, and more — classic-RPG variety, grounded in Edo.
- **§1.5.2 village web** — approved (keep). **§1.5.4 interrelation/allegiance** — approved (keep).
- **§1.5.3 origin — ADD THE FATHER** to faction-3's family (currently mother Oyuki / sister Okimi /
  employer Denbei / friend Kanta / sweetheart Ohana / guild — but no father). Rest approved.
- **Designing this now via a workflow** (`estate-and-combat-redesign`): estate ladder + estate growth +
  earlier canvas + combat content (mobs/quests "far and wide"). Output → revised §1.5 estate + a combat
  brief for §2/§4/§5. Holding §1 edits to apply with the full round-2 batch.
- **§1.6 — Influence model + TIER RENUMBERING + per-tier-transition story gates (human).**
  - **House Influence = ACHIEVEMENT/MILESTONE-driven, NOT a passive rate.** Must NOT slowly tick up over
    time based on assets/estate size; must NOT be a flat per-action increment. It jumps on **achievements
    & milestones of the Samurai Estate**, including **repeating milestone results** (a week's harvest,
    seasonal results). "Sounds complicated — implement carefully & balanced." (Refines old §1.6
    'recognition' framing → explicitly: discrete chunks on achievements + periodic estate results, never
    a trickle, never per-action flat.)
  - **TIER RENUMBERING (0-indexed):** **T0 Estate** (starter/tutorial) → **T1 Village** (opens a lot) →
    **T2 Region** (**INCLUDES the origin family/old-friends @ Sawatari-juku + Kuzuhara**) → **T3 Domain/
    Castle-town** → **T4 Edo** (national). (Old T1–T5 → new T0–T4; remap the world-expansion doc's
    T3/T4/T5 → T2/T3/T4.)
  - **Each tier transition = a MAIN STORY segment / gate:**
    - **T0→T1:** enough estate work + complete **basic repairs** → the estate sends you out into the village.
    - **T1→T2:** **"clean your room"** — estate healthy, village happy, immediate fires out → the lord
      first believes impact beyond the estate/village is possible → quest to grow **regional influence**.
      The Region introduces **RIVAL POWERS — 2+ other samurai houses with more regional influence** you

    - **T2→T3:** **WIN the region** — the other houses are no longer the regional leaders → the
      **castle-town rulers confer that your house leads the region** and you are **recognized & INVITED to

      influence at the **NATIONAL** level; you must "conquer the castle-town" / grow influence (multiple
      ways) to reach **T4 Edo**.
