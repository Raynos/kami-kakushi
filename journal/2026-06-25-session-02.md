## Key decisions (human-locked this session)
- **Grounded, no magic** (D-001): mundane cause for the amnesia (flood + head trauma); no tengu/spirits/
  reincarnation/isekai.
- **Pure Edo folk-mystery tone** (D-002): folklore is *believed-atmosphere only*; every yokai resolves
  to a human/natural cause; thin capped ambiguity, never player magic.
- **Mediocre-start protagonist** (D-003): no hidden power/talent/training; growth only through
  perseverance/grind. Systems guardrail: returning memories/habits grant ZERO mechanical bonus.
- **One grounded diegetic reset** (D-004): a season-cycle time-skip carrying meta-progress; not
  reincarnation.
- **Working title** (D-005): *Kamikakushi*.
- **Process:** plan & review the PRD for the whole saga, section-by-section, full-detail-upfront, before
  building. (See memory `review-decisions-before-building`.)
