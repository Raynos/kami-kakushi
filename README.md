# KamiKakushi : An Incremental RPG game.

> **Note — this is the preserved original seed prompt, kept as a historical artifact.** It is not
> the current canon and some details have since evolved (e.g. the protagonist's age). For the
> authoritative present-day vision see [`docs/living/prd.md`](docs/living/prd.md) §1, and for what is actually
> built see the milestone tracker in [`docs/living/roadmap.md`](docs/living/roadmap.md). The setting is now
> **anchored to 1780** (Edo-era Japan, *An'ei* 9 — real year, fictional geography & politics; ADR D-105). The
> original seed text is preserved unchanged below.

The goal is to build a web based HTML incremental RPG game. 
Feel free to use HTML5 & Typescript in the implementation.

For inspiration, look at proto23 and yet another idle rpg.

The goal of a true incremental game is that the UI is incremental itself, that the first minutes, and hours of the gameplay show a limited set of the UI. As you progress in the story, the game, the level ups you unlock more of the UI elements and features of the game.

Each incremental RPG game has a story, a campaign, things to do, main quest, side quests, mobs to grind on, areas to explore, equipment to find, equipment to craft.

For our story I will seed you a hint.

 - It's based on EDO era japan, 
 - You start out as a ~18 year old farmhand, who wakes up on a samurai estate in the country side. 
 - You don't quite know how you got here, but that's the fun, you can speak to the members of the estate, and someone from the estate will guide you.
