# Part 2 · Build v0.3 of the game (TypeScript)

> **Scope — the whole of T0: re-baseline T0-M1/M2, then build T0-M3 + T0-M4 back-to-back.** v0.3 ships **all four
> T0 milestones to a current-PRD standard**, in three movements:
>
> 1. **Re-baseline T0-M1 & T0-M2 (audit → review → re-implement).** v0.2 was built against an **older PRD**; parts
>    of T0-M1/M2 are **unimplemented, implemented wrong, or implemented with corner-cutting / laziness**. So the
>    shipped slice is **NOT** trusted as-is: we **audit it fun-slice-by-fun-slice against the current `prd/` +
>    `roadmap.md`**, write up the gaps, and **re-implement** what's wrong or missing (not a blanket "keep + retune"
>    — re-implement where it fails the bar). The genuinely-correct parts carry forward; this **supersedes the old
>    blanket carry-forward framing of DS#19/OQ-4** (carry-forward is now *earned per fun-slice by the audit*, not
>    assumed). The T0-M2 HP-carry combat retune (D-050) folds into this re-baseline.
> 2. **T0-M3 — the spine (the #1 gap).** Close the first four-pillar **macro loop**: stand up the live **Estate
>    (家産) pillar** (accrual + seasonal high-water judge) and the **BIG ceremonial manual-opt-in T0→T1 ascension**
>    (grade-gate, grade-scaled boon, tier 0→1 stored, schema bump + dev save-wipe) — proving the spine actually
>    **CLOSES** — built on the re-baselined R0–R3 extended *thinly* to an R7 capstone.
> 3. **T0-M4 — the showcase breadth.** Fill the R4→R7 rungs *around the proven spine* (first quest, the LINEAR koku
>    flywheel taste, the tiny market + mentor lore-talk, the small walkable T0 map, stance/ability reveals,
>    found/crafted 2nd weapon).
>
> **T0-M3 and T0-M4 ship together because they're coupled and intertwined** — the spine needs R4–R7 to climb, and
> M4 is exactly what makes those rungs worth climbing; shipping them apart would leave a release with a hollow
> ladder. But **spine-first holds WITHIN the release** (decision #18 / OQ-2 preserved): M3 is built thin and
> proven to **close** *before* M4's breadth lands.
>
> **Gated on** Part 1's code-affecting decisions. ADRs let it start now (build-first / D-022) at the cost of
> liquid balance numbers possibly re-tuning after playtest — acceptable if balance stays single-sourced in `balance.ts`.

### Carry forward vs. re-baseline — what's trusted, what's audited

> **The shipped v0.2 slice is NOT assumed correct.** It was built against an older PRD, so the T0-M1/M2 *content &
> behaviour* below is **audited against the current PRD before it's trusted** (see *Movement 1* in the build
> steps). What carries forward unconditionally is the **architecture / infrastructure** — that's PRD-faithful by
> construction and the spine plugs straight into it. What's **audited** is everything the player actually
> experiences in T0-M1/M2.

**Trusted — architecture/infrastructure (carries forward, no rewrite):**

- **Pure-core spine:** `reduce(state, intent)` + `tick`/`advanceClock`, immutable structural-sharing `GameState`,
  the universal `applyRewards` bus (whose comment already reserves a Phase-2 `pillarDeltas` hook), the write-once
  unlock/revealPass latch (the *UI-unlocks-incrementally* signature). The spine plugs in — no rewrite.
- **RNG:** seeded splitmix64 with named substreams (combat/loot/**seasonal**/worldgen) + `deriveDayKeyed()`.
- **Clock:** derived `clock.{tick,day}` → season/week/year/lunarPhase (selectors) — the season-boundary judge hooks straight on.
- **Save layer:** multi-backend + crash-recovery + an ordered-forward `migrate()` chain (registered-steps map currently empty→identity) + `migrate.test.ts` exercising the contract. The v0.3 bump registers the first real step.
- **DEV harness:** `window.__qa` already exposes `toRung()` (a teleport precursor), `newGame`, `faceWolf`, `fight`, `auto`.
- **Process gates:** 9-gate parallel `verify` (~1.7 s), `pacing:check`, `playcheck` ratchet, content-verifier, the mandatory `diverge` UI gate, milestone-integrity manifest — all green; v0.3 slices attach DoD instruments here.

**Audited — T0-M1/M2 content & behaviour (re-implemented where it fails the current-PRD bar):**

- **Labour ladder (T0-M1):** `accrueRungMeter` + `promoteRungs` AND-gate, threshold map, R0–R3 authored,
  discover-by-doing skills, stamina gates, the diegetic mentor (Genemon, T0-M1-F3), the minimal SFX/juice pass
  (T0-M1-F4). `phaseOf()` already keys on `rung==='R7' && flags['t0-capstone']` — the contract exists; R4–R7
  aren't authored. **Audit:** does each T0-M1 fun-slice match its current-PRD DoD, or is the mentor/juice/onboarding
  missing or stubbed?
- **Combat (T0-M2):** `resolveFight` race-to-kill sim, analytic + fixed-seed sampled win-rate (20–35% band),
  character level + attribute spend, kendo stance mods, gear/durability/repair, self-recovering soft-setback loss.
  **Audit:** HP-carry/heal-by-eating (D-050) and no-stance-dominated (D-058) — are they actually built, or still
  the old reset-HP / dominance-enshrining behaviour? Is the 2nd weapon found/crafted or still a grant?
- **Economy sinks (T0):** `improve_estate` (koku→stage buffer), `cook_meal`, `spend_attribute` — the linear-koku
  taste (D-066) is *nominally* present. **Audit:** does it actually compound, or is it a dead-end stub?
- **The LOCKED House-Influence teaser panel** (`renderHouseInfluence`) — v0.3 **mutates it in place** into the
  live-Estate + silhouettes view (D-055). **Audit:** is the teaser even wired to real state, or cosmetic?

### Build steps — three movements (re-baseline → spine → breadth)

#### Movement 1 — Re-baseline T0-M1 & T0-M2 (audit → review → re-implement)

> v0.2 was built against an older PRD. Before extending it, **prove what's actually there** with formal audits
> against **both the source code and the running game** — then re-implement the gaps. This is the explicit answer
> to "did we build what the PRD said, or a subset / a half-assed prototype?"

0a. **Source-code audit (PRD-fidelity).** Walk **every T0-M1/M2 fun-slice** in `roadmap.md` + its DoD, and grep
   the actual `src/` for the named instrument. Produce a **gap report** classifying each DoD line:
   ✅ built-correct · 🟡 built-but-wrong/lazy · 🔴 missing. Use the **`battery`** skill (multi-lens fresh-eyes
   stress-test) for breadth; snapshot raw verdicts per the workflow-capture rule. Output →
   `project/audit/reports/2026-06-XX-t0-m1-m2-rebaseline.md`.
   — reads: `content/ranks.ts`, `combat.ts`, `fight.ts`, `pillars`-teaser in `ui/render.ts`, `m1.test.ts`, `m2.test.ts`

0b. **Built-game audit (does it *play* like the PRD?).** Drive the **running** game headlessly
   (`capture-game-states` / `qa-shots.mjs` / `window.__qa`) through the T0-M1/M2 arc — cold open → labour →
   mentor → first wolf → gear/durability — and screenshot/observe each beat. Confirm the *experience* matches the
   PRD (onboarding present? combat humbling-but-winnable? HP actually carrying? juice/SFX firing?), not just that
   the code compiles. Findings append to the same gap report (with screenshots under `project/audit/screens/`).

0c. **Re-implement the gaps.** For every 🟡/🔴 line: re-author it to the current-PRD DoD (the HP-carry retune
   below is one such fix; missing mentor/juice/onboarding, lazy economy stubs, and a grant-not-crafted 2nd weapon
   are others surfaced by 0a/0b). Each re-implementation is a normal verify-green fun-slice with a real test; the
   genuinely-correct slices are confirmed and left alone. **Movement 1 closes when the gap report has zero open
   🔴 and every 🟡 is either fixed or explicitly ADR-amended** (milestone-integrity, D-054).

- **Doc-ripple-before-build rework** — the PRD body is still 5-tier & stale; balance magnitudes are liquid
  (D-059) and may re-tune after the ripple/playtest. Keep them in `balance.ts` so a re-tune is cheap, not a rewrite.
- **Schema bump → save-wipe** — adding `influence`+`tier` (v1→v2) wipes dev/v0.2 saves (intended, D-067). The
  inverse risk is treating it as throwaway: the v1→v2 step must be the **real**, test-covered forward path.
- **Spine-first must hold *within* the release** — even though T0-M4 now ships in v0.3, build the Movement-2 spine
  **thin** (R4–R7 = thresholds + reveal stubs) and prove it **CLOSES** before starting Movement 3's breadth.
  Don't interleave quests/market/map/weapon into the spine steps or the spine-first de-risking (decision #18) collapses.
- **Re-baseline scope-creep (Movement 1 is open-ended)** — "audit & re-implement T0-M1/M2" can balloon into a
  full rewrite. Hold it to the **gap report**: only 🟡/🔴 lines get re-implemented; ✅ lines are confirmed and left
  alone. The audit is **PRD-DoD-bounded**, not a vibes-driven polish pass — scope is "matches the current DoD,"
  not "as good as it could possibly be."
- **Audit theatre (the audit rubber-stamps a half-built slice)** — a code-only audit can miss that a feature
  compiles but doesn't *play*. Mitigated by the **two-pronged** audit (0a source + 0b running-game) and the
  `battery` skill's adversarial fresh-eyes lenses; the gap report must cite a screenshot or a failing/missing test
  per 🟡/🔴, not just an opinion.
- **Silent inert pillar** — `phaseOf()` gates accrual on `R7 && t0-capstone`; if R7's flag isn't wired exactly,
  Phase 2 never opens and the loop is silently dead (the v0.1 write-only-flags bug class). Needs an explicit Phase-2-opens test.
- **Fork retirement re-tunes pacing** — `playcheck`/`pacing` baselines + the ≥30-min floor regression must be
  re-derived under the single real profile, or `verify` goes red on the very commit that retires the fork.
- **Two new/major UI surfaces** trigger the mandatory `diverge` gate (D-073) — budget for the contact-sheet +
  R-item; keep main flag-free (branch-preserved losers).
- **Stance-no-dominated needs HP-carry threaded through both forecast and sim** — or the `displayed==tested`
  invariant (D-057) breaks and gedan stays a trap pick (the retune half-lands and re-enshrines dominance).
