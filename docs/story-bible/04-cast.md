## The MC (partial — human-locked pieces, 2026-07-07)

- **The use-name (locked):** at T0's final rung the house writes him
  down under its **hand-me-down name** — the name every Kurosawa hired
  man has carried, handed down like a tool. *"The house's name for you,
  then. Earn a better."* His name is literally the House's property
  (kernel #4), and earning a better one is the game.
- **The speaker-label ladder (locked):** the log/VN speaker label is
  itself a story surface (kernel #6). Cold open: the first few lines
  read **`You:`** — until the MC asks about his name and Sōan answers
  that he has none; the label flips to **`Nameless:`** on screen, in the
  scene. It stays `Nameless:` through T0 until the use-name beat, then
  carries the use-name. (The cold open needs a small FORCED section
  where you ask for your name — the flip must be witnessed, not
  ambient.) Later evolutions ride the name arc: he KNOWS his birth name
  at T3 (origin tier) and chooses what the register says only at the
  end.
- Sentence shape: plainest voice in the game — short declaratives, no
  figures of speech; a question visibly costs him. *(From the pitches;
  full entry pending the T0 sheet.)*
