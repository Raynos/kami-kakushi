### rake-the-spill

### take-to-the-fields

"The paddies look tended for once," {elder} lets fall at the well, naming no one.

### the-fields-run-on-you

"The fields no longer wait on my orders," {lord} says from the veranda, "they wait on his."

### the-woodlot-runs-on-you

"Even the woodlot answers to him," {drillmaster} says to {elder}, who does not disagree.

### a-granary-against-winter
