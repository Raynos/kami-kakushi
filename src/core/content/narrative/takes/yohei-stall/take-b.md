## prose flavor

### stallGlossFair

the customary rate, as the book records it year upon year

### stallGlossDear

rice gone dear in the hungry months — a season the book marks for
selling

### stallGlossGlut

### stallGlossSagged

### stallRefusal

The pedlar's store will hold no more; custom resumes when his rice has
moved on.

### stallPurseEmpty

again on a market day.
