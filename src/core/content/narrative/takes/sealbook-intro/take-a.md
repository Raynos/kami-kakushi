## prose flavor

### sealbookIntro

For hands kept on, the house keeps a book: small, thin, made to be
carried. {elder} presses the day-hand seal to the first page — 日雇,
vermillion, square and exact — entering the terms from the board the way
he enters anything the house means to keep. An account opened, even of
you.
