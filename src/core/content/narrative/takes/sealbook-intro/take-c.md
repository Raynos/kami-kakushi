## prose flavor

### sealbookIntro

The house puts a thin seal-book into your hands — it keeps nothing it
cannot later point to. The day-hand seal goes down in vermillion, and
the paper takes it without argument. What is pressed today will be read
someday, and the page will answer for you, one way or the other.
