# U1 · R1 the day-hand — VERDICT

## Take A — "the ledger"

What works: the tightest staging discipline (byte-identical ids/effects,
Kihei as the wordless witness — clean under the "…"-react ownership),
and the strongest single arithmetic line: *"Were they still here, you
would not be. They are not."* The day-book close is well judged.

Violations:

- **Epigram saturation (§0.5.2 — Genemon holds NO license).** Three
  maxim-shaped lines in one scene from the man who "has never in his
  life reached for a metaphor": *"A hand laid up in the sickroom is a
  cost, not a hand."* · *"What you were before the weir is not entered
  here. What you do from the gate out is."* (the audit's chiasmus
  template, recurring) · *"A man who asks his terms tends to keep
  them."* Each is a quotable general truth, not item/count/condition.
- **A metaphor in Genemon's mouth (04-cast).** *"Their rows do not know
  it yet."* — personification, from the character defined by never
  reaching for one.
- **Rule 7 — the decide is a morality dial.** The options are literally
  graded dutiful (+1) → practical (0) → ambitious (−1) with Genemon's
  warmth as the standing reward; the option ids even name the axis
  (`r1-dutiful`, `r1-ambitious`). Inherited from canon for
  state-compatibility, but the law is the law — the rewrite wave exists
  to retire exactly this.
- **Speaker legibility (TST4).** `Naoyuki (steward):` — a wrong role
  tag on the second son; the staging note says it resolves via
  `NAMES.heir`, so the visible label and the resolution disagree.
- Naoyuki's restatement *"Two men left us. Do not be a third."* is
  punchy-theatrical rather than "flat and cold" — a warning growl, not
  the abandoned-formality shape 04-cast assigns him.

## Take B — "the held breath"

What works: the most law-clean prose of the three — no true maxims, the
witness dramatized concretely (the book turned to face the kitchen,
*"O-Hisa. You heard the terms."*), the debt named sideways exactly per
stage 1 (*"the house's coin has older claims until autumn"*), Genemon
answering by writing (*"reads the entry back to himself, finds it
already plain, and lets it stand"*), and Shinnosuke's two stacked
questions perfectly in shape.

Violations / weaknesses:

- **Figure-drift in Genemon.** *"You'll see the shape of it by then
  without my drawing it."* — shape/drawing is a reach toward figure
  from the no-metaphor character. Mild, but real.
- **Copy-pasted gesture (the audit's slop pattern).** O-Hisa's *"If the
  rice holds— well. There's tonight."* lifts the cast sheet's
  illustrative sample (*"If the rice holds — well."*) near-verbatim.
  The bible's example is a palette, not a line bank; pasting it is the
  copied-gesture defect.
- **Warmth front-loaded (kernel #3 tension).** *"a bowl already filled,
  and it was filled before the terms were done"* — warmth the work
  hasn't yet paid for, at the tier's coldest rung. Defensible (meals
  ARE the terms) but it softens "arithmetic, not kindness," which is
  the rung's whole point.
- **Crowding vs restraint (§0.5.1).** Four speakers at R1 (Genemon,
  O-Hisa, Shinnosuke, Naoyuki). All node-legal, all voice-true — but
  the silent rung R2 lands harder if R1 is sparser; the scene does a
  beat more than comfort asks, in the opposite direction from the law.

## Take C — "the weather"

What works: the best spec fidelity of the wave. "Kept by arithmetic,
not kindness" is made literal without being announced: *"The paddies
take six hands at the least. Five sleep here now. You are the sixth. If
a better sixth comes by, the book will say so."* Kernel #7 is
dramatized, not narrated: he hears of the quitting late (*"It is the
first you have heard of any leaving"*), and *"Nobody stops eating. Two
hands lost was a morning's talk; one stray kept is not even that."*
Genemon's fled-debt misread becomes a live scene beat — *"Is anything
owing on you?"* at board volume — and the decide is rule-7 exemplary:
no axis, and the REWARDED answer is silence (`+1 kept-accounts`),
because Genemon privately respects a defaulter working a debt off —
the misread paying out as mechanics, unspoken. Naoyuki's beat is the
purest execution of his shape: formal opening abandoned, restated flat
and cold (*"Two hands left. You were here. That is all it is."*), plus
the reads-true seed (*"He looked at you once, and the once was
thorough."*). The forecourt-compression twist is planted in furniture
(*"an estate's worth of paper on a kitchen table"*). None of it passes
the any-game test in the wrong direction — every line is this game's.

Violations:

- **One epigram + bible ventriloquism in the meals ask (§0.5.2 /
  kernel #6's spirit).** *"A fed hand does a day's work; a hungry one
  is half a hand at a whole board. The house is not feeding you out of
  kindness. It is feeding the work."* — the first clause is a maxim
  from a non-license-holder; the second speaks the cast sheet's own
  descriptor ("by arithmetic, not kindness") aloud through the
  character. The six-hands answer already does this work in counts.
- Minor: *"the once was thorough"* is a shade cute for narration this
  cold; *"It has lived with worse"* personifies the book (arguably
  house idiom — ledgers are alive here — allow).
- Practical, not law: option ids/flags are NEW (per the scout §3.3,
  reader-review only) — wiring will need the id map A didn't; the
  `Naoyuki (lord):` form in the header note doesn't match the body's
  bare `Naoyuki:`.

## THE PICK — take C

C is the only take where the rung's thesis is enacted by the mechanics
and the counts rather than stated: arithmetic-not-kindness lives in the
six-hands line, the misread lives in a question the whole board hears,
and the decide quietly rewards what Genemon privately respects. A has
the right temperature but breaks the epigram budget three times, hands
Genemon a metaphor, and carries a literal morality dial. B is the
cleanest against the letter of the law but the softest against the
spec — warmth arrives early, the bible's sample line is pasted in, and
the crowded board dilutes the rung C and A keep cold. C's single law
break is one ask reply; it redlines in two sentences. A false green on
A or B ships, respectively, the audit's epigram/chiasmus slop or a
softened rung; C ships the spec.

## Redlines before wiring (take C)

1. **Rewrite `r1-the-meals`** — delete the maxim and the
   kindness-thesis; answer in item/count/condition only. E.g.:
   *"Meals are the terms. A hungry hand does half a day and eats a
   whole one; the book prefers the other arrangement."* — or simply
   point back at the count: *"Six hands' work, five men fed. The
   sixth eats. That is the whole of it."* (Author-adjust; the
   requirement is: no general truth, no "kindness" said aloud.)
2. **Trim** *"and the once was thorough"* → end on *"He looked at you
   once."* (the thoroughness is Naoyuki's T0 arc to prove, not
   narration's to grade).
3. Reconcile the speaker form: body `Naoyuki:` vs the header's
   `Naoyuki (lord):` — pick one, mint the `naoyuki` NpcId per the
   migration note.
4. Wrap the long dialogue lines to ~80 chars (soft norm; A and B
   already comply).
