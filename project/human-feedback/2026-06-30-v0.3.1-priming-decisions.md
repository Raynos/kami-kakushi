## Batch 1 — the four plan open-Qs

1. **Build order — STRICT PLAN ORDER, no corner-cutting.** Do the whole plan in
   sequence (Step 1 DEV panel + variant infra → Step 2 full variants per surface →
   Step 3 combat → Step 4 koku → Step 5 map node → Step 7 battery leftovers).
   **Author ALL variants** (D-075 full 2–3 per surface) — the human will happily
   review a fuller v0.3.1 later "with more to review."

2. **Combat log — SUMMARISED.** Every repeatable attrition fight collapses to a
   single outcome line carrying the HP swing + loot, e.g. `⚔️ You bring down the
   boar 猪 — four blows traded, the carcass yields. ✓ (HP 18→9 · +8 koku, +1 hide)`.
   _Agent execution call (surfaced, not blocking): the one-time scripted "face the
   wolf" beat keeps its charged narrative — "summarised" targets auto-grind spam._

3. **Koku sinks — ALL THREE directions.** Lean into (a) repair + craft costs, (b)
   deeper estate upgrades, (c) market depth. Balance the income/sink ratio by
   playtest (liquid/D-059).

4. **Loss stakes — MATERIAL/KOKU SETBACK (refined by batch 2, calls 6–7).** A lost
   fight stops autopilot AND costs you. Harshness = **"real bite"** (~a fifth-to-a-
   quarter of carried koku + some carried materials). No permadeath (PRD no-reset).
   Magnitude liquid (D-059). _Batch 2 sharpened WHAT it hits: **carried**, not
   banked → see call 7._

## Batch 2 — the three expansions (the real design session)

5. **MAP = the spatial spine (expands Step 5 / D-078 far past "one node").** The
   map stops being semi-decorative; **location gates everything**:
   - **Every activity belongs to exactly one node. No "default" node.** Today all
     labour shows on the Work tab regardless of `state.location`; that ends —
     you must BE at a node to do its activity.
   - **The start node is wherever you wake & rake rice** (the kura / grain store).
   - **Same activity can appear on multiple nodes with richer yields** — only where
     it makes sense (don't force it).
   - **A new deed/standing source lives on its own node** (walking → standing).
   - **Enemies physically live on nodes** — you can't open a "combat screen" and
     fight everything; you travel to where a foe is. Some nodes have **zero**
     foes, some have **several**. (This is the load-bearing breadth of D-078.)
   - _Playtest risk to watch (not a blocker — human intent is canon, D-022):_
     walking friction in an incremental loop. Tune node adjacency / travel cost so
     it deepens "grounded estate," not tedium.

6. **Combat deadliness — "over-reaching only," with teeth (Step 3).**
   - **HP accumulates across fights** — there is **no free between-fight regen**;
     only eating (D-050) / rest mends. Fight any enemy that deals **≥1 dmg** 100×
     back-to-back and you **die**. The **only** foe safe to auto-grind forever is a
     **0-dmg** one. (The current model already carries HP — this just confirms it
     and kills the auto-loop's auto-heal.)
   - **Two auto-combat modes** (replacing the single auto toggle):
     - **Auto-fight [no end]** — fights until you die or stop it.
     - **Auto-fight [auto-retreat @20%]** — disengages on any **turn** where HP
       < 20%. Crucially a **per-turn** check: a burst foe that 2-hit-kills you
       takes you 100%→~50%→dead before the <20% retreat ever fires — retreat
       guards a grind-down, **not** a glass-cannon. Retreat is a **third fight
       outcome** (win / loss / fled): no reward, no loss-penalty, but you're hurt.
   - _Optional future to explore (NOT default): **stat-driven in-fight HP regen**_
     (would let you sustain vs low-dmg foes; must be driven by a stat, e.g. Vigor).

7. **THE BANK — carried vs banked resources (new, Step 3; ties to call 5).** The
   loss penalty (call 4) hits **carried** resources, so banking must exist:
   - Resources split into **carried** (on you, at risk) and **banked** (safe).
   - **Deposit/withdraw at the storehouse (the kura / home node)** — which, under
     call 5, means you physically return home to bank your haul.
   - A lost fight drops a slice of **carried** koku/materials; **banked is
     untouched.** Risk/reward: bank before a far, risky fight, or carry and gamble.
   - _Implementation note: this is a real state-model change (resources → a
     carried + banked pair, a `deposit`/`withdraw` intent, UI at the kura node).
     Lands in Step 3 (the loss penalty needs it); Step 5 makes deposit spatial._
