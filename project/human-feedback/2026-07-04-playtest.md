**Verbatim:** _"Cold open: the title card sits with an empty box below it for
several seconds — the 'Open your eyes' button only fades in after the intro

the first drained capture). Seed 20260626, `location: kura`, `rung: R0`,

headlessly and tracked the "Open your eyes" button's computed opacity —
