## What this tells us

- **Top of the iOS field: Moonlit (4/5) and Lacquer (4/5)** — both survive the

- **Vermillion re-enters on mobile.** On desktop it was "out of contention"
  (R9, 6→3 cut); on iOS Safari it earns 3/5 — driven by its **chat-log
  one-line-expand-to-full-screen** interaction, not its overall loud direction.
- **The chat-log expand is a real mobile win.** Both 3/5s (Vermillion, Washi)
  are scored *for that specific interaction* — a signal that the collapsed-log →
  full-screen-log gesture is worth carrying into UI-v2 on mobile, independent of
  which visual direction wins.
- Reinforces [[two-good-uis-over-one-compromised-responsive]]: iOS Safari is
  being judged as its own experience, not a responsive afterthought of desktop.
