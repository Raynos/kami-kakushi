## Addendum — what the misses actually were, and the fixes (2026-07-08)

The blind pass is a PROXY (PH5: it proves the ABSENCE of a read, never the
presence; the human is the arbiter of the look). Reading the description
against the misses:

- **V3 (ruin) — real, now addressed.** The reader recovered the *scale* ("the
  estate's outer walled footprint alone is markedly larger than the entire
  village combined") AND the *corner twist* ("the small Guest house annex
  appears carved out of / abutted onto the corner of the larger compound";
  "why does a guest house need its own enclosure separate from its manor?").
  What it did NOT read was **ruined** — my rubble stipple read as "garden
  gravel / a strewn courtyard," and the reader said plainly *"nothing reads as
  a fresh ruin in the sense of broken/collapsed walls being drawn."* Fix:
  broken standing-wall stretches (dry-brush runs with gaps + rubble spill) now
  ring the precinct west + south, so broken masonry — the exact thing the
  reader flagged as absent — reads at fit. Also: the T2 deep-zoom shot is
  repointed at the estate (was the village), so a close look at the ruin exists.
- **V5 (same world as T1) — harness artifact, not a drawing gap.** This inline
  run gave the reader ONLY T2 images, so it could not compare to T1. The
  committed `map-blind-pass` runner hands the T1 fit image over for exactly
  this line; a run through it would not have this gap.
- **V6 (roads) — partly real, addressed.** The reader DID see the estate→village
  road ("a dashed diagonal road crossing from upper-right down through the
  village area") but was unsure if it was a road or a property line, and read
  the estate's and village's roads as separate. Fix: roads now carry a
  continuous silver working-line under the dash (bolder, reads as ONE route end
  to end), and the estate's own NW-upstream + east-over-hill exits are drawn.

**Known-hard line:** the estate-within-ruin twist (V3 here, R5 on T0/T1) is the
line this blind proxy is weakest on — the HR-12-approved T0/T1 sheets fail it
too on the same reader. The human is the arbiter of whether the twist lands;
review live via `?t2-map-demo`. Not re-scored after the fixes by deliberate
choice (proxy cost vs. its known weakness on this line); the drawing changes
target the reader's exact stated gaps.
