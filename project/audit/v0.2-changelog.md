## Rough edges noted for the human (tuning, not blockers)
- **Durability × the close-duel curve:** at combat L1 with no wood, the carrying-pole wears to **Broken** (×0.55 atk) after ~20 fights, which — under the new tighter math — can drop even the monkey below winnable until you go cut wood and repair. Recoverable (woodcutting is unlocked), but the early grind has a friction point. Candidate tunes: gentler early wear, auto-repair in the auto-loop when wood is available, or a small starting wood stock. *Provisional balance — flagged for playtest.*
- **Stance vs Balanced on the wolf:** at some levels both Guarded and Aggressive beat Balanced (a damage-race artifact). Interesting depth, but Balanced being dominated on a foe is worth a tuning look.
- **Koku rewards unchanged + slower XP** → the grind is intentionally longer; the cheapest knob if it feels grindy is `COMBAT_XP_BASE 40→~32`.
- **Stance pulled forward to R3** (PRD's canonical slot is R5) — a documented, reversible pull-forward so the combat decision exists *now*; should get a one-line ADR.

## Post-re-score fixes (an adversarial re-score workflow verified v0.2 + caught these)
An independent 5-agent re-score (`project/brainstorms/raw/2026-06-27-v02-rescore.json`) verified the fixes are
real in code+tests and produced the score deltas (below), and caught real issues — all now fixed:
- **`0bf2d5d` [HIGH] — seed-robust forecast.** The displayed win-rate was coupled to the *run seed* at n=48, so
  monkey@L1 read 0.33 at seed-1 (the test's seed) but **0.48 at the shipped seed** — i.e. the guard test
  protected the wrong experience (the *same* "test guards a number players don't see" flaw the v0.1 audit
  flagged). `foeForecasts` now uses a fixed forecast seed + n=400 → every player sees the true **~0.32**, in the
  signed band; the curve bands were re-aimed to the seed-robust reality.
- **`ae20a2d` [MED] — durability soft-stuck.** Under the tighter curve a Broken pole drops monkey@L1 to ~0%, so
  an idle auto-loop with no wood could grind at 0% forever. The auto-loop now auto-repairs when wood allows and
  **stops** (rather than silently grinds) if Broken with no wood.
- **`ae20a2d` [LOW] — legacy-save false notice.** Additive might/guard/vigor absence no longer flags `coerced`,
  so a v0.1 save loads without a false "we mended a problem" notice; the no-dead-value ratchet now covers the
  attributes.
- Correction: the woff2 font **binaries ARE vendored** (committed in `e756f56`, ~90KB+254KB) — the "deferred"
  note below was wrong; only the *subset/optimization* step is optional. The combat numbers in this doc's
  "Playtest-verified" column above were the old run-seed values; the **seed-robust** numbers are now in row #1.
- Still open (tuning, for the human): the **Balanced (中段) stance is dominated on some foes** at some levels
  (a damage-race artifact — worth a tuning pass); the slower XP grind; and the durability early-friction even
  with the guard.
