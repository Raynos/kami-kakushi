### Task 1 — rice storage costs something (D-118 §1)
- `src/core/content/balance.ts` — added `RICE_SPOILAGE_NUM/DEN` + `riceSpoilage()`
  (per-season decay, integer fixed-point, ≈10%) and `KURA_RICE_CAP_BASE/PER_STAGE`
  + `kuraRiceCap(estateStage)` (U0 120 → U4 440). Magnitudes liquid (D-059).
- `src/core/step.ts` — `onSeasonTurn` fires in `onDayRollover` on each
  `day % DAYS_PER_SEASON === 0` boundary; spoils carried AND banked rice (each
  floors independently), emits an ephemeral "some rice spoiled" Now line.
  Deterministic → fold-invariant (engine.test still green).
- `src/core/intents.ts` — `deposit` caps RICE at `kuraRiceCap(estateStage)`
  (only rice; coin banks freely); a full kura is a no-op.
- `src/ui/render.ts` — storehouse shows `stored X/N rice`, disables the rice-store
  button when the kura is full (title points at improving the estate), blurb notes
  spoilage + the cap.
- `src/core/economy.test.ts` — new tests: spoilage decays both piles by exactly
  `riceSpoilage(each)` on a season turn (and nothing mid-season); deposit caps at
  `kuraRiceCap(0)`, a full kura deposits nothing. Both derive from the source of
  truth → RED against the old free/lossless/uncapped kura.
