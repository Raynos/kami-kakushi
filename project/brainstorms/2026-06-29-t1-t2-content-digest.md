# T1 — Estate (full)

## Frame / fantasy
Graduated the tutorial; **"the real grind"** — the full estate, no longer in miniature. *The mediocre-start
protagonist becomes a genuinely capable estate man.* This is where the **≥30-min/rung floor binds for the first
time** (D-049, D-056; T0 is floor-exempt). Difficulty stays **humbling throughout** (D-061) within winnable /
soft-setback-only / no-stranding guardrails. Every showcase-in-miniature system from T0 (D-052) **scales into
real depth** here. The estate's domain is still its own land — the valley does not open until the Arms ascension
fires at the end of T1. The "first errands past the estate gate / valley comes into view" beat is the **T1→T2
teaser**, not T1 content.

## Rungs
- **~R8 → R15 — ~8 new rungs, ~16 estate rungs total** (proposal; D-052 + reshape; resolves OQ-6).
- **Largely TO-AUTHOR.** PRD's estate ladder ends at **R7 Bailiff** — no canonical R8→R15 copy. Design as
  "full-estate" roles deeper than Bailiff but still estate-scale (before any village).
- Promotes on the **Phase-1 AND-gate**: per-rung-reset rung-meter ≥ threshold AND the rung's story milestones
  (`§1.6.4`, `§2.15.1`, `§4.1.1`). Two sub-meters: **Estate Service** (labour) + **Combat Rank** (martial).
- Thresholds **back-solved from ≥30-min floor × eligible-activity rate** (`§4.1.1`); T1 per-rung floor ≈ **≥40 min**.
- The capstone rung **OPENS Phase 2** (the pillar grind); **pillar DEEDS accrue only in Phase 2** (FU7).

## The pillar — Arms 武威 (NEW this tier)
- **Added at T1; its ascension unlocks the Village (T2)** (D-048).
- **Earned via recognised martial DEEDS only** — a road declared safe; a nest cleared; the grain store defended;
  a rival's enforcer broken — + **seasonal security judged results** on a new high-water (`§1.6.1`, `§2.16`,
  `§4.2.1`). Arms is **distinct** from the two other combat tracks (the likeliest regression): **one kill →
  character level**; **one recognised deed → Arms**; **one curated rung activity → Combat Rank rung-meter**.
- Deeds accrue **only in T1's Phase 2** (FU7).
- **Deed bands** (PRD "T0→T1" 2-pillar analog — stale label): Arms good **0.5K**/great 0.72K/excellent 0.95K;
  per-deed cap 0.04·good = **20 ip**. Estate good **0.8K**/great 1.1K/excellent 1.5K.
- **Ascension gate (T1→T2):** two-pillar hybrid **1 GREAT + 1 EXCELLENT** across Estate + Arms, no overflow
  (D-049). Un-greys the 2nd pillar silhouette (D-055), dream beat, opens the Village.

## Systems introduced / deepened
1. **Koku flywheel BRANCHES** (D-051, D-066, D-008). T0 = single LINEAR line; **T1 branches into three capped
   sub-engines**: **LAND** (*shinden* reclamation), **TREASURY** (debt→solvency, *goyōkin*), **TRADE** (routes,
   broker standing, *meibutsu* — **hard-capped ≤⅓ of Estate & Wealth**, D-008). `G-NO-DEAD-VALUES` now guards a
   compounding consumer.
2. **2nd combat line + 3 T1 weapons** (`§4.6.9`): the **Sword line (Line 2)** on a Combat-Rank rung-gate,
   found/crafted: **Kodachi** (spd 1.25, Riposte), **Uchigatana** (1.10, Iai-draw crit), **Ōdachi** (0.90,
   cleave 2). Signatures deepen at L25/L50.
3. **HP-carry / stance = a real decision** (D-050): HP carries + heals by eating; defensive vs aggressive
   genuinely trade; **no stance dominated**. Couples combat ↔ food/cook sink. (`§4.6`.)
4. **Drill-yard training track**: curated combat activities feed the Combat-Rank meter; conditioning stays the
   zero-stat enablement gate; small bounded per-skill combat perks (`§2.7.1`, D-027).
5. **≥30-min/rung FLOOR binds** (D-049, D-056; `§4.8`): on rung-meter points, back-solved; M6 regression fails
   on undershoot only. Proposal makes the floor regression test go live at T1-M1.
6. **Deeper labour/skill web** + component crafting deepens (simple → component/quality chains, `§2.11`).
7. **Estate physical growth** (`§4.7.5`) — visibly mends (flavour, not a management sim).

# T2 — Village (Asagiri)

## Frame / fantasy
The Arms ascension has fired; the estate trusts the protagonist to **carry its business past its own gate into
the valley.** Frame: *the HOUSE rising* — standing climbs friendly → TRUSTED, reach grows across village + estate
+ roads. This is the **reputation WEB** tier: Asagiri's chief, shops, artisans, inn & rumours, shrine, and the
***kamikakushi* legend** open **around** the estate spine. The spine stays the estate ladder; **the village web
NEVER gates the climb** — ignoring it is poorer-and-lonelier, never a wall (`§1.5.2`/`§1.5.4`). The
**Tama-vs-farmhand allegiance** goes live (Sayo names him "Tama" on sight). **Rival samurai houses appear** at
the capstone (the T2→T3 teaser).

## Rungs
- **~8 rungs (old V0→V7).** No explicit labels in the coarse proposal; reshape keeps a fresh ~8-rung ladder per
  tier (D-012). Old Valley ladder (`§1.5.1`, `§3.4`, `§5 T1.2`):
  - **V0 Errand-runner for the house** — first valley errands; opens market/coin (one shop); seeds the rep web.
  - **V1 Recognised hand** — chief's house + inn & rumours board; valley pest clears.
  - **V2 Road-warden** (combat gate) — make a valley road/the ford safe. *(PRD opened Line 2 here — moved to
    NEW-T1; see ambiguity #4.)*
  - **V3 Steward of the valley economy** — the silk/sericulture *meibutsu* begins (Onatsu); broker meters; TRADE.
  - **V4 Trusted of the headman** — **Foreman Magobei's** skim surfaces; **first Office standing → Office reveals.**
  - **V5 Sworn man-at-arms** — valley watch; DEFEND; first paid retinue; the **Naoyuki shoulder-to-shoulder
    rivalry→respect beat**.
  - **V6 Right-hand-in-waiting** — authority across the valley; levers pointing at the region.
  - **V7 Agent of the house, valley anchored** (capstone) — opens Phase 2; clearing the 3-pillar gate opens the
    region map + rival houses.
- Per-rung floor re-derives (old Valley ~40 min; ambiguity #5).

- **Ascension gate (T2→T3):** three-pillar hybrid **1 EXC + 1 GRT + 1 GOOD** across Estate + Arms + Office
  (D-049). Un-greys 3rd pillar, dream beat, opens Region.

## Systems introduced / deepened
1. **Reputation WEB** (`§1.5.2`, `§2.15`) — unlike the ladder: continuous multi-node meters, gentle curves for
   frequent small dopamine. **Per-shop patron standing** (smith Gonta, broker, herbalist Obaa Kuni, brewer
   Tokuemon, weaver Onatsu), **per-family goodwill**, **artisan/guild standing** (gates recipes/components), **the
   Chief's regard** (Yagōemon).
2. **A real market / TRADE in earnest** — shop row + coin + the silk/sericulture *meibutsu* chain (Onatsu),
   trade ≤⅓ preserved (D-008). (Coupled to T1 ambiguity #2.)
3. **Branching NPC dialogue** (D-039) — `choices[]`/`locksLineIds[]`/flags; data-not-script, deterministic;
   carries Peaceful/Assertive routes + Tama-vs-farmhand leaning.
4. **Inn rumours board + yokai-debunk quests** (`§1.10`, `§2.13`) — optional folklore, **none gate the spine**,
   each resolving one-to-one to a human/natural cause with dawning dread (no Scooby unmasking). Belief→cause:
   "kappa"=undertow/smugglers; "tanuki"=Magobei's skim; "fox-fire"=hidden charcoal kiln (seeds T2, resolves T3);
   "ghost-bird"=a heron. The **one capped residual-ambiguity token** = the jizō-at-the-weir offering.
5. **Rival houses introduced** (`§1.11`) — **House Tomita** (money) + **House Akagi** (honour) appear at the
   capstone; light per-pillar rival stats (overtake computed, not scripted). *Climax (G5/G7) is Region/T3.*
6. **Contested deeds** — the rival contest makes Office/territory deeds contested.
7. **Cross-pillar combos surface** (D-031, `§4.3.1`) — deeds satisfying two domains credit both pillars; computed
   post-trade-clamp, excluded from gate-check + trade denominator. *(Onset-tier conflict — ambiguity #2.)*
8. **Diegetic-mentor onboarding deepens** (D-063) — village/region cast extends Kihei's in-world teaching.
