# Moonlit × Lacquer fusion — 7 concepts, ranked to a spanning trio

**2026-07-03 · discovery capture.** The human is torn between two of the six
`ui-demos/` remasters — **01 Moonlit** (PSX-classic, cool) and **04 Lacquer**
(premium woodblock, warm) — and asked for **3 fusion variants** to build as demos
**07–09** so they can feel which one pulls. This file is the raw idea fan-out +
the ranking; the build spec is the plan
[`docs/plans/opus-2026-07-03-ui-demos-07-09-moonlit-lacquer.md`](../../docs/plans/opus-2026-07-03-ui-demos-07-09-moonlit-lacquer.md).

Method: a 7-way blind Opus fan-out (one agent per *organizing principle* for the
fusion, so the ideas span axes instead of converging on "cool-top/warm-bottom") →
one taste judge scoring all 7 and picking a **spanning, mutually-distinct** trio.
Raw verbatim: `project/brainstorms/raw/2026-07-03-moonlit-lacquer-fusion-ideation-wtw1qor6c.json`
(git-ignored).

## The two parents, in one line each

- **01 Moonlit** — cool night. Indigo→charcoal gradient *menu-boxes*, double-bevel
  keylines, moon-silver text, a **45° gold diamond-crest cursor**, a **left
  vertical menu-rail**, condensed sans, pale-gold numerals. A 1998 JRPG menu.
- **04 Lacquer** — warm dark. Urushi-brown *trays*, fine **gold maki-e keylines**,
  **vermillion seal-stamp CTAs**, cream **paper wells** (the log is ink-on-paper,
  grained), serif prose, a top gold-inlaid tab strip. Octopath premium.

They are near-opposites on **temperature** (cool↔warm), **material**
(night-box/glass ↔ lacquer+paper), **nav** (left-rail ↔ top-strip), **type**
(all-sans ↔ serif+sans), and **signature interaction** (diamond cursor ↔ vermillion
seal). That opposition is what makes the fusion space rich.

## Chosen trio — spanning three orthogonal fusion axes

Not the top-3 by score — a **spanning set**, so each demo teaches a *different*
lesson about what the fusion even means, and they read as three distinct overall
temperatures at a glance:

- **07 · Andon** — **warmth is a PLACE.** Both palettes coexist, spatially zoned:
  a warm lamplit desk (you act) beside a cool moonlit window (the world
  remembers). Warm-forward. *Base 04.*
- **08 · Night Seal** — **warmth is YOU.** The whole house is cold indigo-lacquer;
  the only warm thing is the vermillion seal-cursor that travels with your
  attention. Cold with one red actor. *Base 01.*
- **09 · Damascene Duet** — **warmth is transcended into a THIRD ground.** Neither
  night-box nor paper — a blackened-steel bimetal where silver and gold carry
  fixed meanings. Neutral-dark jewel-box. *Base 04.*

Ordering (07→08→09) is a deliberate gradient: *both coexist* → *one dominant with
a traveling accent* → *neither, a third thing.*

**Deliberately excluded on the spanning rule, not on quality:** Sumi Tide (the
honourable-mention radical end — the pick if the human later wants the boldest
option) overlaps Andon's screen-wide temperature axis and is the riskiest build;
Coldpress overlaps Andon's seam *and* clusters with Night Seal on base-01;
Moongilt / Lantern Rail add no new axis.
