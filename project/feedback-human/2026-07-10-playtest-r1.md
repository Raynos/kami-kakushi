## The Zone tab (IA)

### FB-332 · rename Work → Zone; zone actions + NPC talk live together — ✅
**Verbatim:** _"I'm thinking we rename \"Work\" to \"Zone\" ; that's a big one.
/ Then we move all zone actions into this tab, including talking to NPCs."_
**Reading:** the tab is the PLACE you stand — its labour, its people, its
conversations — not just its labour. The who's-here 衆 list (ADR-114) sat on
the Map tab, splitting "act here" from "talk here".
**Human ruling:** "Do it now."
**Fixed in:** tab label `Work` → `Zone` (no kanji pairing yet — 場 is available
if wanted); the who's-here section + talk-to-reveal wares (pedlar market gate)
moved from the Map pane to the Zone tab, before the market pane in render order
so a departed person still closes their wares. Map keeps travel only.
**Distilled rule:** one tab per capability (TST1) — the zone's actions and its
people are ONE capability: being somewhere.

## The clock

### FB-333 · season + weekday, no year, no day counter — ✅
**Verbatim:** _"In terms of the season, yes we can render Winter, but I don't
want to render the year, nor the day counter. / Just Winter and the day of the
week, not day 4 but Thursday or whatever is canon ( what day of the week was
day 0/1 ? )"_
**Reading:** the absolute count is bookkeeping; the player lives by the week
(market days pull the wheel). Canon didn't define day names or a day-0 anchor.
**Human ruling:** 月..日 kanji+English pairing, **day 0 = Monday 月** — so
Yohei's market days (weekdays 2 & 5) land Wednesday 水 + Saturday 土.
**Fixed in:** `DAY_OF_WEEK_NAMES` canon in `core/constants.ts` (beside
`dayOfWeek`); the clock reads `❄️ 冬 Winter · 月 Monday`; `Year N · day N` is
gone. Test pins the anchor + the no-counter law.

## The body meter

### FB-335 · "does this render satiety or body or stamina?" — ✅
**Verbatim:** _"Does this render Satiety or body or stamin what is this ?"_
**Reading:** the picked span is the header body bar's fill — a bare 4–6px
strip with a small `body` label, no numbers, no hover. TST4: the player never
guesses state.
**Fixed in:** the body meter now carries the SAME exact-number readout life
has (`42/100`, rounded) + a hover title — _"Body 体 — work draws it down; a

"satiety" remains internal-only.
**Distilled rule:** a meter without a number and a name is a mystery strip —
every header vital reads value + identity at a glance (TST4).
