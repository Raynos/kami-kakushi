## The clock

### FB-333 · season + weekday, no year, no day counter — ✅
**Verbatim:** _"In terms of the season, yes we can render Winter, but I don't
want to render the year, nor the day counter. / Just Winter and the day of the
week, not day 4 but Thursday or whatever is canon ( what day of the week was

**Reading:** the absolute count is bookkeeping; the player lives by the week
(market days pull the wheel). Canon didn't define day names or a day-0 anchor.
**Human ruling:** 月..日 kanji+English pairing, **day 0 = Monday 月** — so
Yohei's market days (weekdays 2 & 5) land Wednesday 水 + Saturday 土.
**Fixed in:** `DAY_OF_WEEK_NAMES` canon in `core/constants.ts` (beside
`dayOfWeek`); the clock reads `❄️ 冬 Winter · 月 Monday`; `Year N · day N` is
gone. Test pins the anchor + the no-counter law.
