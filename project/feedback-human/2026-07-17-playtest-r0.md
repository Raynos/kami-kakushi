### FB-411 · rake clickable at zero strength — ✅
**Verbatim:** _"Even when im out of strength and need to rest a moment
I can still click Rake the spilled rice. that's broken / ALl the
actions buttons shoudl be disabled and not usable if Im out of
strength"_
**Reading:** the FB-265 gate was actually firing at capture time — the
button carried `disabled` + the "Out of strength — rest a moment."
reason, and the reducer refuses on the same predicate (AC-6), so no act
could run. The real defect was legibility (TST4): a disabled `.verb` at
opacity .7 on a dark steel plate was indistinguishable from the live
"Rest a moment" plate under it, so it read as clickable.
**Distilled rule:** a dead control must read dead at a GLANCE, not on
hover — dimming alone doesn't survive a dark plate; change the plate's
construction (flat fill, dashed border), not just its alpha.
**Doc-update plan:** candidate button-state recipe for `ui-design.md`'s
M2 button section if the dashed-ghost treatment survives review.
**Fixed in:** `1d4f52b4` — `.verb[disabled]`: flat `steel-1` fill, no
silver rim, dashed border, opacity .45; the title tooltip keeps the
why.
