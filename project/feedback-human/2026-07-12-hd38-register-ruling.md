## The rulings

### D1 — the audience + the clarity floor → **adopted, floor as an OUTCOME test**

The human took *"Outcome test, no quota"* over the plan's proposed per-scene
device ration.

The agent's argument, which the human accepted: the device quota (one
inversion, one litotes, one verbless fragment per scene) **misdiagnoses**. The
disease is **inference load**, not grammar. *"Water first, always."* is a
verbless fragment and perfectly clear; *"You are learning the house's true size
by what it will let itself be seen without."* is a well-formed sentence and the
hardest line in the audit. A quota is also unenforceable (no lint can judge it)
and gameable (spend your one inversion, feel licensed). The teeth is the
**blind paraphrase pass** instead — a check that can actually go RED (PH3).

**Scope:** *"Binds everything now"* — the floor is a prose law, tier-agnostic;
all new fiction from today is authored to it. Only the *re-voicing work* is
scoped to T0.

### D2 — Genemon's two voices → **adopted**

Book voice (what he reads off the page / writes into it) keeps *item, count,
condition*; man voice (explaining, instructing, negotiating, answering) is
plain complete sentences.

**The disambiguation that came out of this fork.** Asked which text *"Genemon
at the R0 rung-up"* actually meant, the human answered: **the Terms speech**
(the R1 beat, which fires at the R0→R1 rung-up) — **not** the R0 reward lines.
That re-ranked the whole work plan and killed the audit's
flip-R0-to-second-person recommendation.

### D3 — the MC's inner line → **adopted, bounded**

Attention and intent only — what he notices, weighs, wants, fears, decides not
to say. **Never memory** (recall is the dream's job, §0.5.4 — the metronome to
T3; an interior line that lets him remember cannibalizes the dream, the T3
reunion, and every misreading in the cast). **Things, counts, and withheld
actions — never adjectives about feelings.** No per-scene quota.

**Person, settled in the same breath:** *"you = experience, he = overheard."*
Second person for all narration and interiority; third person reserved for the
house talking *about* him, until R7 writes his name.

### D4 — scope → **"Worst-first, Then a full sweep"** *(the human's words)*

Not either/or: the five targeted waves clear the acute pain, then a full-T0
sweep carries the rest of the tier to the floor so no register seam survives
between old and new text.
