## 2026-07-07T09:20:50+0200 — so-all-actions-in

So all actions in the game, I no longer like the idea of pressing the button and it just doing the action.

I prefer now for ALL actions to be timed actions,

You press the button and the timer starts, an action like "Rake the spilled rice" takes 5 seconds. all actions take some number of seconds or even minutes for larger actions

The button is then disabled for the lifetime of the action, until it completes. 

There will be a small progress bar under the button or just within the border of the button at the bottom showing the time.

once an action completes, it enters some kind of cooldown state, maybe 2s 

The button progress bar then goes back right to left during the cooldown state, once the cooldown is complete you can press it again to do the timed action.

Pressing the auto button would then do that action in a loop basically, it would still auto press it, it would basically say on cooldown complete go again.
